Why Choose Us


Jain College of Engineering (JCE), Belagavi was started under the umbrella of Jain Group of Institutions (JGI) headquartered in Bangalore. JCE made its beginning in the year 2010 in the disciplines of Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering as well as Mechanical Engineering. All the courses are affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi and our institute is also affiliated to AICTE, New-Delhi.
